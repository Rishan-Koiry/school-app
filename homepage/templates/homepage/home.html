About Our School

RK FUTURE School was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education that prepares students for the challenges of the future while instilling strong values and a sense of social responsibility.

Our educational philosophy centers around holistic development, combining academic excellence with character building, creativity, critical thinking, and technological proficiency to create well-rounded individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our admission process consists of the following steps:

  1. Complete the online application form available on our website.
  2. Pay the application fee of $50 (non-refundable).
  3. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, recommendation letters).
  4. Schedule and attend the entrance assessment test.
  5. Participate in an interview with our admissions committee.

Applications are processed within 2-3 weeks. For more details, please visit our Admissions page or contact our Admissions Office at admissions@school.edu.

Our standard school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The day is structured as follows:

  • 7:30 AM - 8:00 AM: Campus opens for early drop-off.
  • 8:00 AM - 8:15 AM: Morning assembly.
  • 8:15 AM - 12:00 PM: Morning classes with short breaks.
  • 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM: Lunch break.
  • 12:45 PM - 3:00 PM: Afternoon classes.
  • 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM: After-school activities and clubs (optional).

Please note that kindergarten has a half-day option with pickup at 12:30 PM. The school calendar with holidays and term dates is available on our parent portal.

Yes, we offer safe and reliable transportation services covering most areas within a 15-mile radius of the school. Our fleet of buses is equipped with GPS tracking, seat belts, and air conditioning. Each bus has a trained attendant to ensure student safety.

Transportation fees vary by distance:

  • 0-5 miles: $800 per year.
  • 5-10 miles: $1,000 per year.
  • 10-15 miles: $1,200 per year.

For route information and to register for bus service, please contact our Transportation Office at (555) 123-4567 or email transport@school.edu.

Our school follows an internationally recognized curriculum that combines elements of the International Baccalaureate (IB) program with national standards. Our approach focuses on:

  • Inquiry-based learning that encourages critical thinking.
  • Strong foundation in STEM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics).
  • Comprehensive arts and humanities program.
  • Multilingual education with options for Spanish, French, and Mandarin.
  • Character development and social-emotional learning.

Students in grades 11-12 can choose between the IB Diploma Programme or Advanced Placement (AP) courses. Our curriculum is regularly reviewed and updated to meet global educational standards while preparing students for top universities worldwide.

We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities that help students develop their talents and interests beyond academics:

Sports: Soccer, basketball, swimming, tennis, track and field, volleyball, and cricket.

Arts: Orchestra, choir, band, drama club, visual arts, photography, and dance.

Academic clubs: Debate, Model UN, robotics, coding, mathematics, science olympiad, and journalism.

Service and leadership: Student council, community service club, environmental club, and peer tutoring.

Most activities meet 1-2 times per week after school from 3:15 PM to 4:45 PM. Some competitive teams may have additional practice sessions or weekend competitions. Activity fees vary by program, and financial assistance is available for eligible families.
